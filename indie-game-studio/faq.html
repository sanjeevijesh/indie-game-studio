Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our games and services

General Questions

What types of games does GameCraft Studios develop?

We specialize in indie games across various genres, including Metroidvania, Roguelike, Platformers, and Action-Adventure games. Our focus is on creating unique, story-driven experiences with innovative gameplay mechanics.

How can I stay updated on new game releases?

You can subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media (Twitter, Discord, YouTube), or wishlist our games on Steam to receive notifications about updates, releases, and special announcements.

Are your games available on multiple platforms?

Yes! We develop for PC, Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch. Availability varies by title, so check each game's specific platform support on our games page or Steam store.

Purchase & Support

Where can I purchase your games?

Our games are available on Steam, Epic Games Store, GOG, and console marketplaces (PlayStation Store, Xbox Store, Nintendo eShop). Links to all platforms are provided on each game's page.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies depend on the platform where you purchased the game. Steam offers refunds within 14 days of purchase if you've played less than 2 hours. For other platforms, please refer to their specific refund policies.

I'm experiencing technical issues. How can I get help?

Please visit our Discord server's support channel or contact us through the Contact page. Include your system specifications, game version, and a description of the issue. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Community & Development

Can I become a beta tester?

Yes! We regularly recruit beta testers from our Discord community. Join our Discord server and follow the beta-testing channel for announcements about upcoming opportunities.

Do you accept game proposals or collaboration requests?

We're always interested in hearing from talented developers and artists. Please send your portfolio and proposal to our business email through the Contact page. We review all submissions but can only respond to those that align with our current projects.

Are you hiring?

We post job openings on our website and social media when positions are available. Current opportunities are listed on our About page. You can also send your resume and portfolio for future consideration.

Technical Information

What are the system requirements for your games?

System requirements vary by game. Each game's Steam page lists minimum and recommended specifications. Generally, our games are optimized to run on a wide range of hardware, including older systems.

Do your games support controller input?

Yes! All our games support both keyboard/mouse and controller input. We recommend using a controller for the best experience, especially for platformers and action games.

Will you add modding support?

Modding support varies by game. Some titles include Steam Workshop integration and mod tools. Check each game's community hub for specific modding capabilities and guidelines.

Still Have Questions?
