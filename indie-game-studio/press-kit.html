About GameCraft Studios

GameCraft Studios is an independent game development studio founded in 2019, dedicated to creating unique, story-driven gaming experiences. Based in San Francisco, our passionate team of 15 developers, artists, and designers focuses on delivering innovative indie games that push creative boundaries while maintaining accessibility for players worldwide.
