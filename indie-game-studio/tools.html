Tools & Technologies

Cutting-edge technology powering our game development

Our Tech Stack

At GameCraft Studios, we leverage industry-leading tools and technologies to bring our creative visions to life. Our carefully selected tech stack enables us to create high-quality games efficiently while maintaining the flexibility to innovate and experiment.

From powerful game engines to sophisticated design tools, every technology we use is chosen to enhance our workflow and deliver exceptional gaming experiences to our players.

Game Engines

The foundation of our game development

Unity

Our primary engine for 2D and 3D game development. Unity's versatility and extensive asset store make it ideal for rapid prototyping and cross-platform deployment.

Cross-Platform C# Scripting Asset Store

Unreal Engine

For high-fidelity graphics and complex gameplay mechanics. Unreal's Blueprint system and rendering capabilities help us create visually stunning experiences.

AAA Graphics Blueprint C++

Godot Engine

An open-source engine perfect for 2D games and lightweight 3D projects. Godot's scene system and GDScript provide an efficient development workflow.

Open Source Lightweight GDScript

Art & Design Tools

Creating stunning visuals and compelling designs

Adobe Creative Suite

Photoshop for textures and 2D art, Illustrator for vector graphics, and After Effects for animations and visual effects.

Photoshop Illustrator After Effects

Blender

Our go-to 3D modeling and animation software. Blender's powerful features and open-source nature make it essential for creating game assets.

3D Modeling Animation Texturing

Substance Painter

Industry-standard tool for texturing 3D assets. Substance Painter helps us create realistic materials and PBR textures efficiently.

PBR Textures Smart Materials Real-time View

Audio Production

Crafting immersive soundscapes and music

FMOD Studio

Advanced audio middleware for implementing adaptive music and dynamic sound effects in our games.

Adaptive Audio Middleware Real-time

Reaper

Digital audio workstation for recording, editing, and mixing game audio and soundtracks.

DAW Mixing Mastering

Audacity

Free, open-source audio editor for quick edits and sound effect processing.

Audio Editing Open Source Effects
