Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical game project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple mobile game might take 3-6 months, while a full-featured PC/console game could take 12-24 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

What platforms do you develop for?

We develop for PC (Windows, Mac, Linux), mobile (iOS, Android), and major consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch). We can also advise on the best platforms for your specific project.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes! We work with clients worldwide and are experienced in remote collaboration. We accommodate different time zones and use modern communication tools to ensure smooth project management.

What's your pricing structure?

Our pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer both fixed-price contracts for defined projects and hourly rates for ongoing work. Contact us for a customized quote.

Can you help with just one aspect of development?

Absolutely! Whether you need art, programming, audio, or any specific service, we're happy to contribute to individual aspects of your project while you handle the rest.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, we provide post-launch support including bug fixes, updates, content additions, and performance optimization. We can tailor a support package to meet your needs.
